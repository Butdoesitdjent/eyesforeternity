Hidden Idol Series: Career and Job

How can a job and a career become an idol? Well, it can become an idol when we start putting our desire to move up in a company and climb that career ladder or make more money for promotions, above seeking God and His Word. And in fact, takes more of our time in our pleasure, satisfaction, and enjoyment than time with God. Some people may try to climb that career ladder and move up in their company or their job or trying to gain further promotions that acquire more wealth. Or have people desire them or their skill assets more than time with God. They care more about what people think of them in their physical position than they do in their spiritual position. This in fact can become an idol.
