HIGHLIGHTS

David Wilkerson was the senior pastor of Times Square Church in New York City. During his lifetime, David initiated Teen Challenge to help teen gang members, drug users, etc recover and hear the word of God. He is perhaps best known for his book "The Cross and the Switchblade". His prophetic, uncompromised, anointed teaching lead many to life in Jesus.

Disclaimer: we do not support the teachers at Times Square Church since David's passing.
